###### ONLINE SAFETY CHECKLIST:

\-Do not click on suspicious links, messages, or emails
\-Always check website URLs before entering details  
\-Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
\-Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
\-Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible  
\-Be careful while using public Wi-Fi networks  

###### UPI / FINANCIAL SAFETY:

\-Never approve unknown UPI payment requests  
\-Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources and websites

\-Report cybercrime at: cybercrime.gov.in
